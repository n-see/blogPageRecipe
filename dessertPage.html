Totally not sketch Oreo Pizookie

The Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • granulated sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Flour
  • Corn Starch
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Double stuf Oreo
  • Mini chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Cream the butter and sugars together until combined
  2. Add in the eggs and vanilla and whisk to combine
  3. Mix in the dry ingredients, fold in the chopped-up Oreos and mini chocolate chips and spread on a greased skillet
  4. Top with more mini chocolate chips and chopped up Oreos and bake in skillet
  5. Let the cookie skillet set for a little, then top with ice cream and serve
  6. Enjoy!
